The Mission

Over $3 Million Raised for Children's Charities

The FOG RALLY is an annual event produced by the Ferrari Owners Charitable Foundation (FOCF) that has become Northern California's premier charity gathering for Ferrari owners and enthusiasts since 2011.

Every dollar raised benefits Make-A-Wish Greater Bay Area — where our $2.4 million in donations have granted over 250 wishes for children with critical illnesses — and the FOCF Fund for Kids, which provides direct grants to local organizations helping kids in need and youth at risk.

This is the eleventh consecutive year Make-A-Wish Greater Bay Area has been a FOG RALLY beneficiary. The Rally has become one of Make-A-Wish's most successful external fundraising events in the nation.

The Event

Four Days. One Mission.

Year-Round

Pre-Rally Private Events

Throughout the year, FOG RALLY hosts sponsor-partnered private events for our Ferrari owners and enthusiast community. These exclusive gatherings are an opportunity to bring our members directly to you — building relationships in intimate, high-impact settings before the Rally even begins.

Thursday, September 24

The Gala & Fundraiser

150–200 guests at the Fairmont San Francisco. Rally Ferraris in full livery on the red carpet. Charity partner presentations, a lively auction, and fund-a-need paddle raise. The event host sponsor welcomes guests and receives top-level signage and staging.

Friday–Sunday, September 25–27

The Drive & Rally Weekend

30+ Ferraris depart San Francisco on a curated route to the Allegretto Vineyard Resort, Paso Robles. Scenic stops, a Rally lunch, test drives, group excursions, themed dinners, a celebration party, and a farewell breakfast. Three days where sponsors can activate unique brand experiences for a captive, high-net-worth audience.
